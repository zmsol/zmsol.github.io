# Operational Benchmarks: What Good Looks Like

**Most firms don't know if their processes are fast or slow because they've never seen anyone else's numbers.**

This doc lists real operational benchmarks for common professional services workflows. Compare yours. If you're significantly slower, there's waste to remove. If you're at or below the benchmark, you're fine — spend your time elsewhere.

---

## Core Benchmarks

| Process | Slow | Average | Fast | Best-in-Class |
|---------|:----:|:-------:|:----:|:-------------:|
| Client intake (form → file setup) | 5+ days | 2-3 days | 1 day | 2 hours |
| Proposal creation (intake → send) | 8+ hrs | 4-6 hrs | 2 hrs | 45 min |
| Monthly client report | 10+ hrs | 5-8 hrs | 2-3 hrs | 1 hr |
| Invoice generation (time → send) | 1 day | 4 hrs | 45 min | 5 min |
| Compliance filing | 3+ days | 1-2 days | 6 hrs | 2 hrs |
| New client onboarding | 2+ weeks | 1 week | 2 days | 4 hrs |
| Data reconciliation (monthly) | 2+ days | 1 day | 4 hrs | 30 min |

---

## What Separates Best-in-Class

Firms in the "Fast" or "Best-in-Class" column share three characteristics:

1. **They capture data once.** Client enters information one time. Every downstream process (proposal, engagement letter, file setup, invoice) pulls from that single source. No re-entry.

2. **They template everything.** Proposals, reports, letters, filings — each has a structured template with variable fields. No starting from scratch. No searching for last month's document to use as a base.

3. **They separate creation from formatting.** Data aggregation and document assembly are automated. Humans only touch the content that needs judgment.

## The Benchmark Gap Calculator

For each process, calculate your gap:

**(Your time − Benchmark time) × Frequency per year × Blended hourly rate = Annual waste**

### Example: Client Reports

| Your time | Benchmark | Gap | Frequency | Rate | Annual Waste |
|:---------:|:---------:|:---:|:---------:|:----:|:------------:|
| 6 hrs | 2 hrs (Fast) | 4 hrs | 240/yr (20/mo) | $75/hr | $72,000 |

**If you're at "Average" and want to reach "Fast," the gap is worth closing when annual waste exceeds $15,000.**

## How to Read Your Benchmarks

### If you're at "Slow"

You likely have manual processes, no templates, and data stored in multiple places (email inbox + spreadsheet + notes app). The fix is a structured intake and document generation system. Expect 60-80% time reduction.

### If you're at "Average"

You have some structure (templates, basic automation) but data is still being re-entered or documents require significant manual formatting. The fix is connecting your existing systems so data flows automatically. Expect 40-60% time reduction.

### If you're at "Fast"

You have good systems. Further gains come from edge cases and exception handling. The ROI is lower here — only invest if the remaining gap is costing more than $20K/year.

## The Real KPI: Not How Hours You Work, But How Quickly Work Moves

The firms that grow fastest don't work more hours. They have shorter cycle times. A proposal that takes 45 minutes instead of 4 hours means you can respond to more opportunities with the same team.

Cycle time is the single metric that correlates with growth. Measure it. Improve it. Watch everything else follow.
