# Document Automation ROI: What It Actually Saves

**Every document your team drafts from scratch is a bet that automation won't pay off. That bet usually loses.**

In a professional services firm, documents are the product. Proposals, reports, engagement letters, case summaries, compliance filings, invoices. They're also the largest source of unbillable overhead. This doc breaks down the math.

---

## The 80/20 Rule of Document Creation

**80% of every document is template or structured data. 20% is unique analysis or judgment.**

Most firms write documents as if every one is 100% unique. They start from a blank page or a previous document that needed heavy editing. The result: senior people spend time on formatting, data entry, and structure — work that adds zero value.

| Document Type | Template Content | Unique Content | Current Time | Automated Time |
|--------------|----------------:|---------------:|:------------:|:--------------:|
| Client proposal | 75% | 25% | 4 hrs | 45 min |
| Monthly report | 85% | 15% | 6 hrs | 1 hr |
| Engagement letter | 90% | 10% | 2 hrs | 15 min |
| Compliance filing | 80% | 20% | 8 hrs | 90 min |
| Case summary | 70% | 30% | 5 hrs | 2 hrs |
| Invoice | 95% | 5% | 30 min | 2 min |

## The Financial Model

For each document type your firm produces regularly, calculate:

**Annual cost of current process:**
(Hours per document × number of documents per year) × blended hourly rate

**Annual cost after automation:**
(Hours per document × number of documents per year) × blended hourly rate

**Annual savings = Current cost − Automated cost**

### Example: Monthly Client Reports

| Metric | Current | Automated |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Hours per report | 6 | 1 |
| Reports per month | 20 | 20 |
| Monthly hours | 120 | 20 |
| Blended rate | $75/hr | $75/hr |
| Monthly cost | $9,000 | $1,500 |
| **Annual cost** | **$108,000** | **$18,000** |

**Annual savings: $90,000**

Cost to build the automated system: $6,000–12,000 one-time.

**ROI: 750%–1,500% in year one. Every year after is pure savings.**

## The Intangible Benefits

Beyond the direct time savings, automated document production gives you:

- **Consistency.** Every client document follows the same format, same structure, same quality bar. No variation between team members.
- **Speed.** Respond to a client request in hours instead of days. That wins business.
- **Scalability.** Take on more clients without adding administrative headcount.
- **Compliance.** Automated systems don't forget steps, skip fields, or use outdated templates.
- **Staff satisfaction.** Nobody joined professional services to copy-paste data between documents.

---

## What Doesn't Get Automated

Not everything should be. The 20% of a document that requires judgment, strategic thinking, or client-specific nuance stays with your team.

Automation takes the 80% that is mechanical and makes it instant. Your team does the thinking. The system does the moving.
