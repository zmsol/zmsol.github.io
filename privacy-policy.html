Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Z&M Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website at zmsol.github.io. This page explains how we handle any information you provide when using this site.

We take privacy seriously. We collect only what is necessary to respond to you, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form on our site, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your company name (if provided)
  • Your message — the details you choose to share about your workflow or needs

We do not collect browsing behavior, cookies for advertising, analytics tracking, or any data beyond what you voluntarily submit through the form.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you submit is used for one purpose only: to respond to your inquiry. This includes:

  • Replying to your message via email
  • Following up on a potential engagement
  • Sending the specific information or proposal you requested

We do not use your information for advertising, remarketing, or any automated decision-making.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Form submissions are sent to a Google Sheet (via Google Apps Script) and stored in Google's cloud infrastructure. Email notifications are sent through standard SMTP. Both services are operated by Google LLC and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not maintain a separate database or CRM of submitted data.

4. Data Retention

We retain form submissions for as long as necessary to respond to and complete the associated engagement. If no engagement results, we delete or anonymize the data within 90 days of the last correspondence.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us. See Section 7 for contact information.

5. Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sheets / Google Apps Script — form submission processing and storage
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting
  • Google Fonts / Font Awesome / Tailwind CSS — front-end resources served from CDNs

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We do not control how these providers collect or use data beyond what we explicitly configure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy or to request access, correction, or deletion of your data:

Email: privacy@zmsolutions.io

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our practices change or as required by law. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
